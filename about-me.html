About Me

A little about me

Hello! I'm Brandon Rosen, also known online as ItzIcyHere or ItzIcyGaming. I'm an 11th grader as of 2024/2025 who is passionate about tech,
coding, and cybersecurity. One of my earlier projects, Shared Unblock Slides, was a major part of my life. It gained significant attention
by providing access to unblocked games and resources at school, and while I still feel a sense of pride in the technical skill it took to
create, I’ve grown to see it as a mistake.

The experience taught me valuable lessons in ethics and responsibility. It showed me the importance of using my skills for good rather than
for rebellion or acts that could be seen as malicious. Since then, I’ve focused on positive growth, such as participating in my cybersecurity
class, contributing to my community through Key Club, and being one of only five students in my state invited to speak on a panel at an AI
Conference. These experiences have shaped my commitment to using technology to create a positive impact.

Now more about cybersecurity!
I have always been interested in tech, but my passion for it really took off when I started my cybersecurity class. I have learned
so much about the importance of cybersecurity and how it can be used to protect people and organizations from cyber threats. I have also
learned about different types of cyber threats and how to defend against them. I am excited to continue learning about cybersecurity and
hope to pursue a career in the field in the future.

Some of the cerficiations I expect are CompTIA Security+, CompTIA Network+, Pentester+, Ethical Hacker Pro & more as I decide.
